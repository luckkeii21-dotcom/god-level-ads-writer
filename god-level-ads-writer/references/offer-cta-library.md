## 1a. Lead Magnet Intelligence — What Works in 2025–2026

_Status: `[PROVEN WINNER]` — 2025 Meta lead gen performance data_

**What has declined:**
- Generic PDFs ("Download our free guide to X") — overused, low perceived value
- Whitepapers in B2B — no longer sufficient differentiation
- "Free ebook" offers — commoditized across all verticals

**What is winning:**

**Utility Tools** (highest-performing category)
- ROI/cost calculators: "Find out exactly how much you're losing to X per month"
- Self-scoring checklists: "The 7-point checklist we run before every [outcome]"
- Quizzes that self-qualify: "Find out which [solution] is right for your situation"
- Key: immediate, tangible value the user can act on now, not after reading 40 pages

**Interactive / Event-based**
- Live workshops and webinars (B2B): consistently outperform gated content for mid-market
- Mini video series (3–5 short videos): perceived value higher than static PDF
- "Office hours" style calls for high-ticket services

**Specificity premium:**
The more specific the lead magnet, the better it qualifies and the lower the effective CPL.
"The 11-point audit we run before any Meta campaign — and the 3 things that kill 80% of
ad accounts" converts better than "Free Facebook Ads Guide" on both CTR and lead quality.

**How this affects ad copy:**
Lead with what the person gets immediately, not with the business behind it.
Headline should name the specific utility: "Get the free ROI calculator" beats
"Download our resource" on every metric. The lead magnet IS the offer — write to it.

## 1. The Hormozi Grand Slam Offer Framework

**The value equation:** Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort)

**To increase conversion:**
- Maximize: Dream outcome clarity + Evidence of likelihood (proof, testimonials, credentials)
- Minimize: Perceived time to result + Perceived effort required from the buyer

**Hormozi's ad copy formula:**
"I help [WHO] get [RESULT] in [TIMEFRAME] without [BIG OBJECTION]"

Examples:
- "I help NJ contractors get their perfect work truck in 24 hours without overpaying at big dealerships"
- "I help women over 40 lose 20 lbs in 90 days without giving up their favourite foods"
- "I help med spas generate 50+ qualified leads a month without spending more on ads"

**Grand Slam Offer Creation:**
1. Define the dream outcome — what do they REALLY want (not what they say they want)?
2. List ALL barriers preventing them from achieving it
3. Create a solution for every single barrier
4. Package + name the solution compellingly
5. Add bonuses that solve adjacent problems

**The MAGIC Offer Name Formula:** Magnet + Avatar + Goal + Interval + Container
- "6-Week Celebrity Smile Blueprint"
- "90-Day Revenue Acceleration System"
- "30-Day Skin Transformation Program"
- "12-Week Commercial Truck Buying Blueprint for NJ Contractors"

## 3. CTA Library by Funnel Stage

### Cold Traffic CTAs (TOFU — Never Heard of You)
Low commitment, high curiosity:
- "Watch this free training — link below"
- "Get your free [specific deliverable]"
- "Download the [guide/checklist/report] — free"
- "See how it works → [link]"
- "Learn more"
- "Watch the demo"

**Rule for cold CTAs:** Never ask for a call or purchase on cold traffic. The ask must be as low-friction as possible.

### Warm Traffic CTAs (MOFU — Engaged, Knows You)
Medium commitment:
- "Book your free consultation — [X] spots this month"
- "Apply now — limited spaces available"
- "Start your free trial"
- "Reserve your spot — free strategy call"
- "Join [X] others — get instant access"
- "Claim your [offer]"

### Hot Traffic CTAs (BOFU — Ready to Buy)
High commitment, direct:
- "Enroll now — next cohort starts [date]"
- "Buy now — [X]% off until [date]"
- "Yes, I want [specific result]!" (first-person CTA)
- "Get started today — [risk reversal statement]"
- "Claim your spot — only [X] left"
- "Schedule now — [urgency reason]"

### Retargeting CTAs (Hot + Specific)
- "You visited us but didn't book — here's your [offer] before it expires"
- "Still thinking about it? Here's what [Client Name] said after week 1"
- "Last chance — your [discount/offer] expires [date]"
- "Only 3 spots left for [month] — secure yours now"

### India-Specific CTAs
- "WhatsApp karein abhi" + number on screen
- "Free consultation book karein — WhatsApp ya call"
- "Abhi message karein, 24 ghante mein jawab milega"
- "Yeh offer [date] tak hi hai — act karo"
- "Pehle [X] logon ke liye FREE hai"

## 4. Urgency & Scarcity Mechanisms

**Critical rule:** Urgency and scarcity must be REAL. Fake urgency destroys trust when discovered, and sophisticated audiences spot it immediately.

### Real Urgency/Scarcity Options
- Limited spots: "I'm only taking 5 new clients this month" (only works if true)
- Limited time pricing: "[Offer] ends [specific date]"
- Inventory urgency (dealerships): "This truck won't last the week" (very credible for physical inventory)
- Cohort start: "Next group starts [date] — enrollment closes [date-2]"
- Price increase: "Prices go up [date] — lock in current rate now"
- Seasonal: "End of summer special — offer expires [date]"
- Bonus expiry: "Get the bonus [X] only if you book this week"

### Urgency Language That Works
- "Before it comes down" (softer, less salesy)
- "For the next [X] people only"
- "This [week/month] only"
- "Won't last long" (physical inventory — very credible)
- "[X] spots left" (most effective when the number is small and true)

### Urgency Language to AVOID
- "Act now or regret it forever" (too aggressive)
- "This offer expires in 24 hours" (if it doesn't, trust is gone)
- Countdown timers that reset (sophisticated users refresh and see it)

## 5. Risk Reversal Types (Ranked by Power)

Adding a visible risk reversal can increase conversion by up to 32%.

| Type | Example | Power Level |
|------|---------|------------|
| Performance guarantee | "If you don't get [result] in [time], you don't pay" | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Money-back guarantee | "100% refund if not satisfied — no questions asked" | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Try before you commit | "Free first session — see results before paying" | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| Keep the materials | "Keep the course even if you refund" | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Cancel anytime | "No contracts — cancel whenever you want" | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| Satisfaction guarantee | "If you're not completely happy, we'll fix it" | ⭐⭐ |

**Hormozi's positioning on risk reversal:**
"Either your promise needs strong risk reversal OR your offer must be so irresistible they'd feel stupid saying no."

**Risk reversal phrasing examples:**
- "I'm so confident in this, if you follow the plan and don't see results in 16 weeks, you don't pay. I'm that confident."
- "Try it for 30 days. If it's not exactly what we promised, get every penny back — no questions, no forms."
- "We'll redo the treatment for free if you're not completely happy with your results."

## 6. Tripwire Offers

Tripwire offers ($7–$47) bridge free leads to high-ticket clients by leveraging commitment/consistency psychology.

**Why tripwires work:**
- A small purchase mentally primes for more — "I've already invested in this"
- Tripwire buyers show 60–70% repeat purchase rate vs. 5–20% for new customers
- Filters out freebie seekers who will never buy
- Qualifies intent without the risk of a full commitment ask

**Tripwire sweet spots:**
- Digital products: $7, $17, or $27
- Service assessments: $27–$47 ("Book a 30-minute strategy session for $27")
- Physical samples: $9.95 + shipping

**A $9 offer converted 41% better than $19 in one niche test** — lower price isn't always the answer, but $9 hits a psychological sweet spot.

**India tripwire pricing:** ₹99–₹499 for digital, ₹199–₹999 for service entry points.

**Tripwire → High-Ticket Funnel:**
$9 PDF / $27 session → $97–$297 course → $997–$3,000 coaching → $10K+ mastermind

**The rule:** Every tripwire must deliver MASSIVE value relative to the price — so the buyer's first thought is "if this is what I get for $27, what does the main program look like?"
